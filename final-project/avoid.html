Danger Zones & Places to Avoid


The Chasm: Underground

The Blocked Path

The Ad-Hoc Main Tunnel is the main entrance to the underground mines. The combination of air filled with fumes, low visibility, and corrosion drom dark mud make it an unsafe location for visitors.


Musoujin Gorge

Rift Valley, Yashiori Island

Located on Yashiori island, Inazuma, this rift was supposedly formed by a blow that ended the life of the serpent god. Lightning lingers in the valley, polluting both the air and water, making the area hazardous to anyone's health.


Amakumo Peak

Where Swirling Stormclouds Gather

Amakumo Peak on Seirai Island, Inazuma, lies under a massive Electro storm that turns the surrounding water into a constant electrical hazard. Though the peak and the storm are breaktaking to see, the area remains dangerous to visit.
