Site Planning

Purpose & Audience

This site will showcase viewpoints in Genshin Impact. It'll include a ranking of based on which are the best to visit if they were real, as well as which to avoid, and a gallery of various viewpoints in the game. The audience includes friends, Genshin players who like the scenery, and anyone curious about the game's visuals.


Site Outline

  1. Home - Intro and overview of the site
  2. Top Attraction - The best viewpoints, ranked in order with reasoning
  3. Worst Visits - Highlights some of the worse viewpoints
  4. Gallery - Various viewpoints organized by region/nation

Design Plan

The site will be predominantly various shades of dark blue to complement the colorfulness of the viewpoints. Majority of the text will be white for readability.

Fonts:

  • Headings will use Rye to match Genshin's style
  • Body text will use Roboto for readability
